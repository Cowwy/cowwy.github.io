Background Summary

  • Robert John Downey Jr. is an American actor. Successful during his early career, follow by a period of substance abuse and legal troubles. After some time, he made is appearance on stage and became one of the most popular actor.

  • He made his acting debut at the age of five back in 1970, appearing in his father's film Pound.

  • In 1992 he starred as title character in the film Chaplin, which earned him a nomination for the Academy Award for Best Actor.

  • Today, he is famous and known as cast for Tony Stark (Iron Man).
