Projects

Pooper Clicker (2019)

An idle game that takes you on a thought-provoking, humorous symbolic journey to unravel the mystery of the eternal poo conflict.

You will be harvesting, collecting and trading poo for upgrades to further advance your ultimate goal of purging the mysterious poo off the face of the planet.

You can play this game by clicking here or visit the github repository.

Built with pure HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript with ES6 features to demonstrate the extend of my ability with JavaScript and DOM manipulation without any 3rd party library.
